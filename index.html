Paycom is a comprehensive Human Capital Management (HCM) software that streamlines various HR and payroll tasks. Whether you’re a large corporation or a small business, Paycom offers solutions to manage the entire employment life cycle, from recruitment to retirement.


Paycom: One Login, One Platform

One of the standout features of Paycom is its unified platform. Instead of juggling multiple programs with separate access credentials, Paycom consolidates everything into a single HCM software. Here’s what this means for you:

  • Simplified Access: No more jumping between different tools. With Paycom, all your HR and payroll functions reside in one place.
  • Employee-Driven: Employees can access their information 24/7 through an intuitive mobile app. From managing paychecks to requesting time off, it’s all at their fingertips.
  • Payroll Approval: Paycom takes it a step further by empowering employees to verify and approve their own paychecks. This reduces errors, compliance risks, and speeds up the process.

How to Log in to Paycom?

  • Visit the Paycom Login Page: Go to the Paycom Login page (https://www.paycom.com) to access your account.
  • Enter Your Credentials: Provide your login credentials (username and password).
  • Navigate Your Dashboard: Once logged in, explore your personalized dashboard. You’ll find features related to payroll, benefits, time tracking, and more.

How to Log in your Account from Mobile Device?

  • Download the Paycom App: Get the official Paycom app from your app store (Google Play Store for Android or Apple App Store for iOS).
  • Open the App: Launch the Paycom app on your mobile device.
  • Log In:
    • Employees:
      • Use your username and password (same as web login).
      • Set up additional security (PIN or fingerprint).
    • HR Professionals/Managers:
      • Log in with your admin credentials.
  • Explore Your Dashboard:
    • Access features like payroll, time tracking, benefits, and more.

How to Reset password?

  1. Open the browser and visit the website: https://www.paycom.com.
  2. Click on Forgot Password option below the credential field.
  3. On the next page, enter the username in the first field.
  4. Provide the last four digits of the social security number in the second field.
  5. Provide the date of birth in the final field and click on Next.
  6. Follow the further instructions to reset the password and regain access to the portal.

Troubleshooting Login Issues

  • Incorrect credentials: Double-check that you are using the correct username and password. If you have forgotten your password, click 'Forgot Password' on the login page and follow the instructions.
  • Locked account: If you have entered incorrect credentials multiple times, your account may be locked. Contact your payroll administrator or HR department to reset it.
  • System maintenance: Occasionally, Paycom may perform system maintenance, which can affect login availability. Check the Paycom website or contact your employer for updates.

Features and Benefits of the Paycom Service Portal

  • The portal allows the employees to check their payrolls, previous and upcoming payments, incentives and much more.
  • The account provides access to expense management, tax payments, W-2 forms and much more.
  • The leaves, attendance, upcoming holidays, schedules, timetables and other information are also available in the portal.
  • Employee benefits such as pensions, health and dental insurance, pay raise and other provisions.
